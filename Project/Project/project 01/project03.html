Cultural Heritage

Sri Lanka's cultural heritage spans over 3000 years, encompassing Buddhism, art, architecture, and literature. The country is home to eight UNESCO World Heritage Sites.

Ancient Civilization

The ancient Sri Lankan civilization was one of the world's greatest hydraulic civilizations, known for its sophisticated irrigation systems and architectural marvels.

Path to Independence

Sri Lanka gained independence from British rule on February 4, 1948, marking the beginning of a new era for the nation.

Modern Sri Lanka

Today, Sri Lanka is a diverse nation known for its natural beauty, rich culture, and growing economy. The country continues to develop while preserving its historical heritage.
