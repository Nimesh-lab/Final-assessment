Ancient Kingdoms

Sri Lanka's history dates back to over 2,500 years with the establishment of the Anuradhapura Kingdom.

Colonial Rule

Sri Lanka was colonized by the Portuguese, Dutch, and British, leaving a rich cultural legacy.

In 1948, Sri Lanka gained independence from British rule, marking a significant milestone in its history.

Modern Era

Today, Sri Lanka is known for its diverse culture, beautiful landscapes, and resilient people.
